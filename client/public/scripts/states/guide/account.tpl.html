Register a new account

  1. Click the login button or in the top right corner
  2. Click either create new account button or link
  3. Fill in the email field, password field, and password confirm field.
  4. Click the I'm not a robot check box. Sometimes reCaptcha will ask to select images as a test.
  5. Once the form is valid, click the register button.

Forgotten password

  1. Click the login button or in the top right corner
  2. Click the forgot password link at the bottom of login form.
  3. Enter your email address into the field.
  4. Once the form is valid, click the submit button.
  5. An alert will show saying an email has been sent to you successfully.
  6. Go to your email client, open the email, click the reset password link.
  7. The link will redirect you back to the website. The reset password form will be visible.
  8. Type your new password once and then twice to confirm.
  9. Once the form is valid, click the submit button.

Account dashboard

Once the resume profile is created the user will be allowed to check in every 24 hours so that their profile is pushed to the top of lists for searches.

Resume profile

  1. Log into your account to access your resume profile. Click either the user menu icon in the top right of the screen and then the resume profile link . Or, the Account → Account dashboard menu item in the side navigation.
  2. From the account dashboard you can create your resume profile.
  3. Click the create button to start making your resume profile listing.
  4. Once your resume profile is created click check in to push your resume profile to top of the list. You can do this once every 24 hours.
  5. If the resume profile is active you can click the deactivate button to prevent it being listed.
  6. If the resume profile is deactivate you click the activate button to start listing it again. If the action is performed longer than 24 hours since the last time of a check in, the resume profile will once again be at the top of the list in searches.
