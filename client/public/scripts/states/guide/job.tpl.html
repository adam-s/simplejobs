Search job listings

  1. Click the search jobs button on the side nav.
  2. Use the pager by scrolling to the page bottom to see more listings.
  3. Open the listings filter by clicking the filter icon in the subheader.
  4. After the listings filter is opened use the job types filter or the job position filter to narrow your search.
  5. It is possible to sort job listings by proximity.
  6. There are several popular ports to choose from in the select location select box.
  7. If your location is not contained in the select location select box, use the Google places autocomplete search to look up your location. Once you select your location, it will update the proximity sort.
  8. Once the filters and proximity sort is chosen, click the submit filter button to activate.

Create a job listing

  1. To create a job listing.
    • Open the user menu and click my job listings or click the my job listings button in the side nav.
    • Click the add button in top right.
  2. Fill out every field in the form. Click the submit button when all require fields are completed.

Manage job listings

  1. Open the user menu and click my job listings or click the my job listings button in the side nav.
  2. To add a job listing click the add fab button in top right.
  3. To edit a job listing click the edit icon button.
  4. Click the filter icon button to filter job listings.
  5. The clear all the filters using the X icon button.
  6. Job listings can be deactivated and made active again. Click the active toggle switch to turn a job listing off or on.
